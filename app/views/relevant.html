What we think 'relevant' means

When a user finds a page that describes some data, how do they determine that it is relevant?

What we think 'relevant' might mean for data

  • Meets expectations for what user expected to see.
  • Matches the type of data that they are looking for. If a user is looking for libraries, they are looking for physical things.
  • Only have the page, and links, to determine if it is relevant
  • We don't know what user expectations are (they haven't sketched it out), but we do know what they do to try and meet them (download the data).
